ABOUT

ishtār van looy (she/they) is a photographer/videographer based on Gadigal country (Sydney) specialising in live music and event photography. She has worked in the Sydney scene for over three years, with experience in both digital and film photography. In addition, she has interests in experimental and non-traditional modes of image capture and display, such as 3D scanning, LiDAR capture, virtual reality, and data moshing. Outside of photography, her interests include electronic music, ceramics, and Near Eastern archaeology.
